What is the date for the 2015 Fremantle Craft Beer and Food Festival?

Saturday 14 November & Sunday 15 November 2015

What are Festival Times?

Saturday: 12.30pm-8.30pm & Sunday: 12.30pm - 8.00pm

Where is the festival?

Esplanade Park, Fremantle.

Where can I buy my ticket?

Tickets are available through our website.

How much are tickets?

Tickets are on sale now. Single day ($27 + B.F.) and weekend tickets ($37 + B.F.) will be available. See our tickets page for further details.

What do I get for the price of my ticket?

All tickets include an official Beerfest cup for use throughout the Festival. Tastings are typically priced $1-2 for 100ml, as decided by the breweries themselves. The master classes, live comedy, music & other activities are included in your ticket.

Will there be gate sales?

Gate sales will be available both days. We recommend getting your tickets online in advance, as they will be slightly cheaper and gate sales will be delayed as the event nears capacity (as happened last year).
